#  Grade Review Committee [two faculty members are required]  

Before April 1st of each year, the chairperson sends to all GWD faculty an email asking them to be a member of the Grade Review Committee in the event that a students asks for a review. Each of the two members will have a designated substitute.

>  __________  Seconds the motion
