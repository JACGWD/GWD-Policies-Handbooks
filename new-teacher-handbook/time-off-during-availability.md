# What if I want to take time off outside of the summer non-availability period?

## For one week or less

Fill out the **Authorization_For_Planned_Faculty_Absence_Form_-__INTERACTIVE_Feb.2017.pdf** and submit to the program dean (currently Amanda Argento).

## For longer than one week

Fill out the **Authorization_For_Planned_Faculty_Absence_Form_-__INTERACTIVE_Feb.2017.pdf** and submit to the program dean (currently Amanda Argento) and then to Academic Dean (currently Charbel Mourad).
