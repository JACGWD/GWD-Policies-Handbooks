# Procedure for the election of department Chairperson(s) and Coordinator(s)

Department chairpersons are coordinators, not supervisors. Usually GWD receives 0.75FTE allocation from the College.

- Must be held before April 1 of each year;
- The term of office is one year;
- The election may be held at a department meeting or by email;
- The division of chairperson release time should be explicit.

## Example of Division of Allocation

- Jane Hannah will receive 0.25 for Program Coordination Affairs
- Jane Hannah will receive 0.25 for Department Coordination Affairs
- Ginette Di Francesco will receive 0.25 IT Affairs & Capital/Operational Budget Affairs.
