# Complaints Received by the department chair
 
 If a student or faculty or employee complains about someone, the steps to be taken are the following:

### Scenario 1

 - Inform student/faculty/employee to talk directly with the person with who they have the conflict;
 - Problem gets resolved.
 - Person must inform chair that the problem has been resolved.

### Scenario 2
 - Inform student/faculty/employee to talk directly with the person with who they have the conflict;
 - If situation or problem does not improve within two weeks;
 - Chair, person and student/faculty/employee will meet to try and remedy the situation;
 - If situation or problem persists, 
  - if faculty, they should be directed to JACFA;
  - if student, they should be directed to Program Dean;
  - if employee, they should be directed to their specific Union.
