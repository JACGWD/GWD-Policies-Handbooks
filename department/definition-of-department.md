# Definition of the GWD Academic Department
 
 The "department" may consist of specialists from the following subdisciplines: such as 
 - UX/UI analysts
 - HTML/CSS coders
 - web site managers
 - video and motion graphics specialists
 - typographers
 - prepress technicians
 - traditional print media graphic designers

 The "department" consists of these employees:
  
- permanent full-time (tenured) teachers
- full-time teachers (without tenure)
- part-time teachers (with semester or yearly contracts)
- part-time teachers (paid à la leçon)
